Contest rules

Lobby and payout format

Players create an account, log in, enter a 5-player or 20-player lobby, draft 9 hitter slots and 2 starting pitchers, wait for the slate to finish, then payouts settle from the lobby payout table.

Five-player lobbies pay two places. Twenty-player lobbies pay seven places. The app scales those payout tables with the selected buy-in.

For hitters, the prototype drafts randomly from a long qualified-hitter pool modeled around 3.1 plate appearances per team game. For pitchers, SP rounds only pull from the day's loaded starters.
